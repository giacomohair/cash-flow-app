How to use My cash-flow

The two views

  • Dashboard — choose the date range and read the summary KPIs: Final EoP, Running Savings and low-balance Alerts.
  • Cash-flow view and full data input — the full weekly table. The collapsible ⚙️ Settings panel holds grouping, horizon, items, alerts and reset, applied to the table below.
  • Weekly data updates — a fast, mobile-friendly screen to update one week at a time (one-off income/expenses, credit-card charges and the actual end-of-week cash).

Add income & expenses

  • In Settings use + Inflow / + Outflow. Turn on Recurring to repeat automatically (weekly, every 2 weeks, monthly, or custom). Outflows are stored as negative automatically.
  • Use 📅 on a row to change its recurrence, 🗑️ to delete it.

Edit the table

  • Click any week cell and type an amount — totals recalculate instantly.
  • BoP (Beginning of Period) is the cash at the start of the week; EoP (End of Period) is the cash at the end (BoP + net flow).
  • You can type the actual EoP of a week directly: the Adjustment row is recomputed so the balance matches reality.

Alerts & grouping

  • Set an EoP Alert threshold; weeks whose EoP falls below it turn red.
  • Switch grouping (Weeks / Months / Quarters / Years) and collapse/expand periods to zoom in or out.

Your data

  • Everything is saved automatically to your private account and synced across your devices.
